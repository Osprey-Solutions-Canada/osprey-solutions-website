Frequently Asked Questions

What are the 5 legs of Google for business?

The 5 Google business products are: Google Business Profile (your map listing), Google Analytics (website tracking), Google Search Console (search performance), Google Ads (paid advertising), and Google Merchant Center (product listings). Together they form the complete Google ecosystem for local businesses.

Why is Google Business Profile verification so hard?

Google has tightened verification requirements significantly. Video verifications get rejected, postcards don't arrive, and suspension notices are confusing. We navigate these protocols daily and get profiles verified, live, and ranking — saving you hours of frustration.

What are backlinks and citations and why do I need them?

Citations are your business listed on directories like Apple Maps, Bing Places, Yelp, and industry sites. Backlinks are other websites linking to yours. Both signal trust to Google. We build 120+ quality citations in your first 90 days to boost your local rankings.

What is Generative Engine Optimization (GEO)?

GEO optimizes your online presence for AI-powered search like ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Google AI Overviews. We structure your data so AI models recognize your authority and recommend your business. This is the future of search and we build it in from day one.

Do I need the Ads package or is management enough?

Management alone works great for businesses that want to dominate organic and map search. If you want to appear at the top of Google immediately for competitive keywords, the To the Top package adds Google Ads and Meta Ads for maximum visibility.

Can I start with setup and upgrade later?

Absolutely. Many clients start with the $499 setup to get verified and optimized, then add monthly management once they see the results. You can upgrade or change plans anytime — no contracts.

What results can I expect and how fast?

Most businesses see improved map visibility within 2-4 weeks. Citation building shows results in 30-60 days. Google Ads generate calls within the first week. Full optimization across all 5 Google products typically produces significant results within 90 days.
