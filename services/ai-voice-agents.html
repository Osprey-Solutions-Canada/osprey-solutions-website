Frequently Asked Questions

How does the AI voice agent work?

When a customer calls, the AI answers in a natural voice trained on your services, pricing, and FAQ. It answers questions, books appointments, qualifies leads, and sends everything to your CRM. Most callers don't realize they're talking to AI.

Does it sound robotic?

No. We use Google's Gemini Live voice model, which sounds natural and conversational. It handles interruptions, follow-up questions, and natural conversation flow. The best way to judge is to call our demo line and hear for yourself.

Can it book appointments on my calendar?

Yes. The AI connects to Google Calendar and offers real available slots. When the caller confirms, the appointment is booked instantly and both parties get a confirmation.

How much does it cost?

Setup starts at $500 with a monthly management fee. Includes a dedicated phone number, custom training, CRM integration, and ongoing optimization. It typically pays for itself by capturing calls you'd otherwise miss.

What CRM systems does it work with?

GoHighLevel (GHL), Jobber, and custom integrations. After every call, the AI extracts the caller's name, phone, service interest, and callback preference — then creates a contact in your CRM automatically.

Can an AI voice agent handle multiple calls at the same time?

Yes. Unlike a human receptionist who can only handle one call at a time, your AI agent handles unlimited simultaneous calls. During busy periods — morning rushes, after storm events, right after an ad goes live — every caller gets answered immediately with no hold times or busy signals.

Will callers know they're talking to an AI?

Most callers don't realize they're speaking with an AI. We use Google Gemini Live, which uses natural speech patterns and handles interruptions conversationally. In jurisdictions that require disclosure, the AI introduces itself appropriately — while still qualifying leads and booking appointments effectively.

Can the AI transfer calls to a real person?

Yes. If a caller requests a human or if the AI determines the conversation needs personal attention, it can warm-transfer the call to your phone. You can set rules — for example, always transfer calls about large commercial jobs directly to you while the AI handles routine residential inquiries.
