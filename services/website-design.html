Why Your Website Design Directly Costs You Revenue

Every 1 second of load time costs you 7% in conversions — that's Google's own research. A typical WordPress site built on a cheap theme scores 35-55 on PageSpeed Mobile. Ours score 90+. At the same traffic level, that speed difference alone can double the number of people who actually contact you. Not through better marketing. Just through a better-built site.

First impressions happen in 50 milliseconds. Before your visitor reads a word, they've already decided whether to trust you. Research from Stanford Web Credibility shows 75% of people judge a company's credibility based on its website design. In competitive trades, the business with the better website wins — even if their prices are slightly higher. Your website is your #1 salesperson working 24/7.

Template websites carry hidden costs beyond poor performance. When every competitor uses the same Divi or Elementor themes, your site looks identical to theirs. Google's ranking algorithms increasingly reward websites with strong Core Web Vitals scores — LCP, CLS, and INP — as direct ranking factors. Custom-built sites pass these tests. Template sites rarely do. You're fighting for the top of search results with a website that Google scores as below average.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a custom website cost?

Standard sites are $3,000-5,000. Premium scroll animation sites are $5,000-7,000. Elite 3D interactive sites are $7,000-10,000. All include SEO, mobile optimization, and hosting setup.

How long does it take?

Standard: 2-4 weeks. Premium: 3-5 weeks. Elite: 4-6 weeks. We can have a working demo live in 24-48 hours for review.

Do you use templates?

No. Every site is custom built from scratch. We don't reskin WordPress themes. Your site will be unique, fast, and built specifically for your business and market.

Will my website work on mobile?

Every site is built mobile-first. Floating call buttons, touch-friendly navigation, fast load times, and responsive layouts are standard on every build.

Do you include SEO?

Yes. Schema.org markup, optimized titles, clean URLs, internal linking, image optimization, XML sitemap, and local SEO setup. We don't build websites that can't be found.

What is a PageSpeed score and why does it matter for my business?

Google PageSpeed Insights scores your website 0-100 on loading performance, specifically on mobile. Scores below 50 hurt your search rankings and cause visitors to leave before your page loads. Every 1-second delay costs 7% in conversions. We guarantee 90+ on every build — most template sites score 30-55 on mobile.

Do you build websites on WordPress?

No. We build custom HTML, CSS, and JavaScript sites using Tailwind CSS and GSAP animations. WordPress plugin bloat kills PageSpeed scores, security vulnerabilities require constant updates, and template themes look identical to competitors. Custom builds are faster, more secure, and completely unique.

Can you redesign my existing website?

Yes. If your current site has good content, we can rebuild it with improved performance, conversion optimization, and updated design. We audit your existing site first, identify what's working, and build a better version that retains your SEO equity while dramatically improving speed, design, and conversion rate.
