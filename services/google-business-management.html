Frequently Asked Questions

How much does Google Business Profile management cost?

Our GBP management starts at $200/month. This includes weekly posts, review responses, photo updates, Q&A management, and monthly reporting. No contracts — cancel anytime.

How long does it take to see results?

Most businesses see improved visibility within 2-4 weeks. Map pack rankings typically improve within 30-60 days depending on competition. We track impressions, calls, and direction requests so you can see progress weekly.

Do I keep access to my Google Business Profile?

Absolutely. You own your GBP — always. We're added as a manager, not an owner. If you ever leave, nothing changes on your end. Full transparency, full access.

What's included in GBP management?

Weekly Google Posts with images, review monitoring and response, photo/video updates, Q&A management, category and attribute optimization, service and product listings, monthly performance reports, and local SEO strategy.

Can you manage multiple locations?

Yes. We manage multi-location GBP accounts for businesses with 2-50+ locations. Each location gets its own optimization strategy tailored to its local market.

How do I get my business into the Google Map Pack?

Google Map Pack ranking comes from three factors: proximity, relevance, and prominence. We control relevance (categories, services, keywords in your description) and prominence (review velocity, posting frequency, photo updates, engagement signals). Consistent active management over 30-90 days typically improves Map Pack positioning significantly.

How important are Google reviews for local SEO?

Very important. Review count, star rating, and how recently you received reviews all factor into Google's local ranking algorithm. Businesses with 50+ reviews and consistent new reviews outrank competitors with fewer. We monitor your reviews daily and respond within 24 hours to every new review — positive and negative.

Can I get suspended from Google Business Profile?

Yes — GBP suspensions happen for violations like keyword stuffing in your business name, incorrect addresses, or policy breaches. We manage your profile in full compliance with Google's guidelines to prevent suspensions. If a suspension does occur, we handle the reinstatement process on your behalf.
