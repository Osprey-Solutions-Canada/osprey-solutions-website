Frequently Asked Questions

How does RoofQuote measure roofs?

RoofQuote uses satellite imagery and building footprint data from OpenStreetMap and Microsoft to calculate roof area. Homeowners enter their address, see their roof on a map, and get an instant square footage measurement and pricing estimate.

How accurate are the measurements?

Typically within 5-10% of manual measurements. It's designed for quick estimates that capture leads — not replace a final on-site measurement. The goal is to give homeowners a real number fast.

Can I embed it on my website?

Yes. RoofQuote is designed to embed on any website — WordPress, Squarespace, Wix, or custom HTML. We provide the embed code and help you set it up.

What information do I get from each lead?

Name, email, phone number, property address, roof square footage, estimated price range, material preferences, and a branded PDF report. Leads are sent via SMS and email in real-time.

Does RoofQuote work for metal roofing and flat roofing too?

Yes. You configure your own pricing rates for each material type — asphalt shingles, metal roofing, cedar shake, flat/TPO, and any custom material you offer. Homeowners select their preferred material and see a price estimate based on your actual rates for that specific material.

Can RoofQuote measure multi-story homes or complex roof shapes?

RoofQuote calculates roof area from the building footprint, which works well for standard residential roofs. For complex shapes, estimates come with a note that a final measurement will be done on-site — which sets proper expectations and still generates the lead effectively.

How does RoofQuote compare to EagleView or GAF QuickMeasure?

EagleView and GAF QuickMeasure are tools contractors use internally for accurate measurements. RoofQuote is a customer-facing lead generation tool that converts website visitors into qualified leads. They solve different problems — RoofQuote runs 24/7 on your website capturing homeowners who are already looking for a roofer.
