Frequently Asked Questions

How is SEO content different from regular blog posts?

SEO content is strategically written to rank for specific keywords. Each article has optimized title tags, internal links, FAQ schema, and is structured for Google and AI search. Regular posts are just content — SEO articles are traffic machines.

How long does it take to see results?

New articles typically start ranking within 30-90 days. We prioritize quick wins first — long-tail keywords and questions your competitors aren't answering — so you see traffic early while building toward competitive terms.

Do you optimize for AI search engines?

Yes. ChatGPT, Perplexity, Claude — we structure content for AI recommendation. Conversational subheadings, FAQ sections, semantic depth, and real author attribution. Princeton research shows +40% AI inclusion from structured content.

How many articles per month?

We recommend 2-4 articles per month. Each targets a specific keyword cluster and links to your service pages. For aggressive growth, 4-8 per month accelerates results.

What topics do you write about?

What your customers are searching for — service questions, cost guides, comparisons, how-to guides, and local content. Every article answers a real question and drives traffic to your service pages.

How long should SEO blog articles be to rank on Google?

For competitive local service keywords, we target 2,000-3,000 words per article. Long-form content ranks better because it covers topics comprehensively, earns more backlinks, and satisfies Google's helpfulness criteria. Short 300-500 word posts rarely rank for anything meaningful in 2025.

What's the difference between on-page SEO and content SEO?

On-page SEO optimizes your existing service pages — title tags, meta descriptions, schema, headers. Content SEO creates new pages targeting keywords your service pages can't rank for — cost guides, comparisons, how-to articles, FAQs. You need both. Our SEO Content Engine handles the content side while ensuring all on-page elements are correct.

Do you write SEO content for industries other than roofing?

Yes. We write SEO content for any local service business — plumbing, HVAC, landscaping, painting, pest control, dental practices, law firms, and more. The strategy is the same: find what customers in your market are searching for, write authoritative articles targeting those queries, and build organic traffic that converts.
