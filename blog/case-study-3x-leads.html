Case Study

How We Helped a Roofer 3x Their Leads in 90 Days

Real results from a real roofing client. Same budget. Three times the leads.

By Osprey Solutions·March 27, 2026·7 min read

The Problem

A mid-size roofing company in the Southeast US came to us with a familiar story:

  • Spending $3,500/month on Google Ads with no conversion tracking
  • Getting 15-20 leads per month but couldn't trace them back to any specific campaign
  • Office phone going to voicemail 30% of the time (owner and one office manager)
  • Google Business Profile last updated 8 months ago
  • No blog content, no SEO presence
  • Competitors consistently showing above them in the map pack

Their frustration: "I know I'm spending money on marketing but I have no idea if it's working."

What We Did

Month 1: Stop the Bleeding

Google Ads Overhaul:

  • Added 66 negative keywords (immediately stopped spending on "roofing jobs," "DIY roof repair," etc.)
  • Restructured campaigns by service type (repair vs. replacement vs. inspection)
  • Created dedicated landing pages for each campaign
  • Installed conversion tracking for phone calls and form submissions
  • Launched Local Services Ads alongside search campaigns

GBP Revival:

  • Updated business description with keyword-rich copy
  • Added 15 new project photos with geotags
  • Responded to 47 unanswered reviews
  • Published 4 Google Business Posts
  • Fixed NAP inconsistencies across 12 directories

Result after Month 1: Lead volume increased from 18 to 29 leads (+61%). Cost per lead dropped from unknown to $47 (now trackable).

Month 2: Amplify

AI Phone Agent Deployed:

  • Configured custom AI trained on their services, materials, and service area
  • Integrated with their GHL CRM for instant lead delivery
  • Set up for 24/7 answering — every call captured

Content Published:

  • 2 blog posts targeting "roof repair in [city]" and "storm damage roofing [city]"
  • Service pages optimized for each city in their service area

Result after Month 2: 38 leads. AI phone agent captured 12 leads that would have gone to voicemail (verified by after-hours timestamps). Blog posts indexed and generating first impressions.

Month 3: Compound

Optimization Phase:

  • Paused underperforming ad groups, doubled budget on winners
  • LSAs generating leads at $31/lead (vs $52 for search campaigns)
  • GBP now showing in top 3 map pack for 4 of 6 target keywords
  • Reviews grew from 89 to 127 (automated request sequence working)
  • AI agent handling 35% of all incoming calls

Result after Month 3: 54 leads. Triple the starting volume. Cost per lead averaged $39.

The key insight: We didn't increase their ad budget. We made the same $3,500/month work 3x harder by eliminating waste, capturing missed calls, and building organic visibility.

What Made the Difference

Three things created the compound effect:

  1. Conversion tracking — for the first time, we could see exactly which campaigns generated calls and which wasted money
  2. AI phone agent — capturing 12+ leads per month that previously went to voicemail was essentially free money
  3. GBP management — moving into the map pack generated organic leads that cost nothing

None of these alone would have tripled leads. Together, they created a system where every channel reinforced the others.
