How Much Does Digital Marketing Cost for Contractors?

By Osprey Solutions|March 2026

Nobody Gives You Straight Numbers. We Will.

Every contractor we talk to asks the same question: "What's this going to cost me?"

And every marketing agency dodges it: "It depends on your goals," "Every business is unique," "Let's schedule a call to discuss pricing."

We're not doing that. Here are the real numbers — based on managing 80+ contractor clients across North America. These are what things actually cost in 2026, not hypothetical ranges designed to get you on a sales call.

Frequently Asked Questions

What's the minimum a contractor should spend on marketing?

$1,700/month — Google Business Profile management ($200) plus Google Ads ($1,500 total including management). This is the minimum to generate consistent leads in most markets.

How do I know if my marketing is working?

Track three numbers: cost per lead, number of leads per month, and close rate. If you can't calculate these, your marketing isn't being tracked properly.

Should I hire an agency or do it myself?

If you have 2-4 hours per week to dedicate to marketing consistently, you can handle GBP and basic social media yourself. For Google Ads, SEO, and website development, the learning curve makes agency management more cost-effective for most contractors.
