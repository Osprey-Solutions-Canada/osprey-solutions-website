What Is an AI Voice Receptionist?

An AI voice receptionist is a phone system powered by artificial intelligence that answers incoming calls in real time. Unlike traditional answering services or voicemail, it:

  • Has a natural conversation with the caller (not a phone tree)
  • Asks the right questions — name, phone number, what service they need, when they're available
  • Sends the lead details directly to your CRM, email, or phone via text
  • Works 24/7 — nights, weekends, holidays, during storms when call volume spikes
  • Costs a fraction of what you'd pay a human receptionist or answering service

Think of it as hiring a receptionist who never calls in sick, never puts anyone on hold, and works every hour of every day for under $200 a month.

Why Contractors Are Switching to AI Phone Agents

The Answering Service Problem

Traditional answering services charge $300–$500/month. They use human operators reading from scripts who often know nothing about roofing, HVAC, or electrical work. The result? Callers feel like they're talking to a call center — because they are.

Common complaints from contractors using answering services:

  • "They couldn't answer basic questions about our services"
  • "Messages were delayed — sometimes by hours"
  • "Customers said the experience felt impersonal"
  • "We were paying per minute and the bills kept climbing"

The AI Alternative

Our AI voice agents are trained specifically on your business. When a homeowner calls your roofing company, the AI knows:

  • What services you offer (roof repair, replacement, inspections, gutters)
  • What materials you work with (GAF, IKO, James Hardie)
  • Your service area
  • Your availability
  • How to handle emergency calls differently from routine inquiries

It doesn't read from a generic script. It has a real conversation, understands context, and responds naturally.

How It Works (Step by Step)

  1. Customer calls your business number — nothing changes for them
  2. AI answers within 2 rings — friendly, professional greeting using your business name
  3. Natural conversation — AI asks about their project, timeline, and contact details
  4. Lead captured instantly — name, phone, email, service needed, and call notes sent to your CRM
  5. You get notified — text message and email with full call summary within seconds
  6. Follow up on your schedule — call them back when you're ready, fully informed

The entire process takes 60–90 seconds. The homeowner hangs up feeling heard. You get a qualified lead without lifting a finger.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does an AI voice receptionist cost?
Our AI voice receptionist plans start at $150/month for contractors. That includes the AI agent, a dedicated phone number, CRM integration, and ongoing optimization. Compare that to $300-500/month for a traditional answering service that can't answer questions about your trade.
Will customers know they're talking to AI?
Most callers don't realize it's AI. The voice is natural, the conversation flows normally, and the AI is trained on your specific business. We've had callers thank "the receptionist" for being so helpful.
What happens if the AI can't handle a call?
If the caller has a complex question or specifically asks for a person, the AI can transfer the call to you or take a detailed message. You set the rules for when and how transfers happen.
Can it book appointments on my calendar?
Yes. We integrate with Google Calendar so the AI can check your real availability and book appointments during the call. No back-and-forth scheduling needed.
How long does setup take?
Most contractors are live within 48 hours. We handle all the configuration — you just need to tell us about your services and how you want calls handled.
