Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate are the roof measurements?
RoofQuote uses a database of 92 million building footprints combined with satellite imagery. Measurements are typically within 5% of manual measurements — accurate enough for estimates, with final measurements confirmed during your on-site inspection.
Can I customize the pricing on the estimates?
Yes. You set your own prices per material type and per square foot. You control what homeowners see. Prices can be shown as ranges or exact figures — your choice.
Does it work on mobile?
Yes. The widget is fully responsive and works on phones, tablets, and desktops. Most homeowner interactions happen on mobile.
What CRM does it integrate with?
RoofQuote integrates with GoHighLevel, Jobber, and email. We're adding more CRM integrations based on client requests.
